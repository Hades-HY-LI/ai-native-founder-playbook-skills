# Pricing And Onboarding

Use this reference to design the first offer and activation path.

## Pricing Principles

- Price against the business outcome or painful workaround.
- Keep early packaging simple.
- Use pilots when value is uncertain but urgency is real.
- Avoid free usage that does not produce learning.
- Capture willingness to pay during sales conversations.

## Onboarding Principles

Activation should lead to the promised outcome quickly.
