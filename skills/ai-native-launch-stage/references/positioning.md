# Positioning

Use this reference to convert product capability into customer value.

## Positioning Frame

```text
For:
Who struggle with:
The product helps them:
Unlike:
It wins because:
Proof:
```

## Message Test

Good positioning:

- names the customer plainly;
- describes a painful workflow;
- promises a concrete outcome;
- avoids vague AI claims;
- distinguishes from current alternatives;
- can be used in outreach and demos.

Weak positioning:

- leads with model technology;
- targets too many personas;
- uses generic productivity language;
- cannot explain why now;
- sounds like a feature rather than a business outcome.
