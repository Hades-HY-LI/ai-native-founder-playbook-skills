# Metrics

Use this reference to separate real product-market fit signals from noise.

## Metric Groups

Track:

- acquisition source and conversion;
- activation to first value;
- retention and repeated use;
- revenue, expansion, and pipeline quality;
- AI quality and failure rates;
- customer support and success signals;
- qualitative customer pull.

## Strong Signals

- users return without reminders;
- customers expand use cases;
- buyers tolerate imperfect UX because the value is high;
- users describe the product in their own words;
- retention improves after targeted product changes;
- sales cycles become more predictable for a narrow ICP.

## Weak Signals

- press, likes, or demo interest without use;
- pilots with no owner or success metric;
- usage driven by founder hand-holding;
- revenue from customers outside the target segment;
- high signups with low activation.
