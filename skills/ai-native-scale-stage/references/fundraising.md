# Fundraising

Use this reference when fundraising is tied to the next scale milestone.

## Narrative Structure

```text
Problem:
Why now:
Customer:
Product:
AI-native advantage:
Traction:
Business model:
Market:
Team insight:
Milestones:
Use of funds:
Risks and mitigations:
```

## Evidence To Gather

- retention and usage;
- revenue or pipeline quality;
- customer quotes;
- expansion signals;
- sales cycle evidence;
- data or workflow advantage;
- team velocity;
- milestone plan.

## Output Format

```text
Fundraising readiness:

Strongest proof:
Weakest diligence area:
Milestones before raise:

```
