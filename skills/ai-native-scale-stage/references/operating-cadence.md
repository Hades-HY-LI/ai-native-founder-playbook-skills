# Operating Cadence

Use this reference to design the management system for an AI-native startup.

## Weekly Cadence

Run:

- metrics review;
- customer learning review;
- product quality and eval review;
- sales and pipeline review;
- hiring and capacity review;
- risks and decisions review.

## AI-Assisted Operating Artifacts

Use AI roles to maintain:

- weekly founder memo;
- customer learning digest;
- product and eval changelog;
- sales objection tracker;
- hiring pipeline summary;
- board or investor update draft.

## Human Decision Gates

Keep humans accountable for:

- strategy;
- hiring decisions;
- customer commitments;
- pricing and packaging;
- product roadmap tradeoffs;
- legal, security, and financial commitments.

## Output Format

```text
Operating rhythm:
Dashboard:
Meeting agenda:
AI-maintained artifacts:
Human approval gates:
Decision log:
Next review date:
```
